Chemistry concept:

It is the science that studies matter and the changes that occur to it, specifically by studying its properties, structure, composition, behavior, interactions and what it causes through it. Chemistry studies atoms and the bonds that occur between them to form molecules, and how these molecules then bond to form matter It also studies the interactions that occur between them.

Its branches:

Analytical chemistry:
Quantitative and qualitative observations are used to determine and measure the chemical and physical properties of materials, meaning that all chemistry is analytical

Physical chemistry:
a combination of chemistry and physics where physical chemists study how matter and energy interact. Thermodynamics and quantum mechanics are important branches of physical chemistry.

Organic chemistry:
It studies compounds that contain carbon in particular. Organic chemistry is defined as the "chemistry of life" because all molecules that make up living tissues contain carbon as a part of them

Inorganic chemistry:
the study of materials such as mi20nerals and gases that do not contain carbon as part of their composition.

Biochemistry:
is the study of the chemical processes that occur within a living organ.
